So, what’s a PNK?

PNK / piː · ɛn · keɪ / noun

Someone who moves to the beat of their own drum—and uses technology, creativity, and craft to make the world more awesome.

The PNK code

  1. 01 Make the world better.
  2. 02 Build it well. Make it beautiful.
  3. 03 Stay curious. Break stale assumptions.
  4. 04 Share what you know.
  5. 05 Never underestimate the value of cool.

The space

Built for the beautifully unfinished.

STEAM PNKS is being imagined as a place where instruction and exploration strengthen each other—where you can take a course, chase your own project, help a friend, or simply hang out near the sparks of other people making things.

Technical excellence matters here. So do aesthetics, play, expression, and the weird little details that make a creation feel unmistakably yours.
